Source: Salesforce, May 11, 2026. 1,688 opportunities across 28 active EMEA AE/SM. Currency converted to EUR at mid-market rates (not finance-grade).

Acquisition (Acq) = New Practice + Reactivate Practice opportunity types. Acq stalled = open Acq opportunity with no stage change in 60+ days. For reference, 90% of NP wins close in under 35 days. The aging strip splits ALL open opps (including Up-Selling) into 0–30d (fresh), 30–60d (warming), 60–90d (stalled), 90d+ (severely stalled). Stage bar shows Consult → Discover → Close → Order Form Sent → Order Form Viewed.

Avg cycle = mean days between CreatedDate and CloseDate for 2026 closed-won opportunities. Close-won rate = won / (won + active losses) where active losses are deals lost within 120 days of creation. Hygiene losses (deals lost >120 days after creation, the Q1 pipeline cleanup) are excluded from both metrics so they reflect 2026 selling activity, not historical bookkeeping. Suspiciously short cycles (under 10d) combined with 100% close-won rates indicate retroactive opportunity creation — Sascha Derichs, Jakub Orłów, Damian Stachowiak, and Rachael Penney all show this pattern.
